General ►

What is My Wishlist? ►

On My Wishlist you can create a wishlist. Easy and for free. Creating a Secret Santa is also possible.

Is Wishlist for free? ►

Yes, you can use My Wishlist at no cost at all.

How do I create a Wishlist? ►

After registering, your wishlist is created immediately. You can then start adding items.

Does my Wishlist have an app? ►

My Wishlist is a "web app", which you can add to your home screen: Apple: In Safari, click the share icon, and select "Add to Home Screen". Android: In Chrome, open the menu, and select "Add to Home Screen".
